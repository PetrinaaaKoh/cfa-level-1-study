## 🚀 **How to Use**

### **Step 1: Open the Study Site**
Click the link above or go to:https://PetrinaaaKoh.github.io/cfa-level-1-study/


### **Step 2: Select a Topic**
- Click any topic from the left sidebar
- Start with Ethics (highest exam weight at 15-20%)

### **Step 3: Study the Content**
- Read the key concepts
- Review formulas (where applicable)
- Study the key points

### **Step 4: Practice Questions**
- Scroll down to practice questions
- Select your answer
- Click "Submit Answer"
- Read the explanation
- Click "Next Question"

### **Step 5: Track Your Progress**
- Click "Mark as Complete" when finished
- Your progress saves automatically
- Check your overall progress at the top
